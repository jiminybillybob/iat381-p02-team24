New Location Opened in Surrey

Been to Surrey recently? The brand new Guildford Town Center looks gorgeous and the team at Tokyo Pizza Club has taken notice. That's why our fourth store location has been opened in the heart of Surrey. Check out our new location right by Red Robin and across from the Guildford Town Center food court.

The Dark Fry

Our newest addition to our constantly evolving menu is the Dark Fry. An okonomiyaki recipe inspired by the food carts on the streets of the Kito disctrict. It has a unique crunch that you will notice due to the extra crispy crust that is part of its cookin process.

2014 Vancouver Critics Awards

Our flagship Downtown Vancouver location has been awarded the prestigious 3 star rating from the Vancouver Food Critics Board. Our famous okonomiyaki recipes is joining the list of great eats around Denman street alongside the likes of Kintaro Ramen and Guu!
