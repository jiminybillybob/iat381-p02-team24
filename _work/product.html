The Shibuya Special

$11.40

Inspired by the city of Tokyo, the “Town of Young People”, the Classic Tokyo uses fresh and local ingredients—picked up daily at the nearby market. This Hiroshima style favourite is made up of layers rather than mixture of ingredients. The layers are batter, cabbage, pork, cheese, yakisoba noodles, and a generous amount of okonomi sauce.

Toppings

  • Tempura bits
  • Japanese mayonnaise
  • Okonomi Sauce
  • Bonito flakes
  • Green onions
  • Fried egg
