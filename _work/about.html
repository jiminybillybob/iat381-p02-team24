About Us

Tokyo Pizza Club was established in 2014 to offer Canadians a taste of the Japanese soul food, Okonomiyakiâ€”a Japanese savoury pancake, but in a more familiar form.

Okonomiyaki is a popular pan fried food that consists of batter and cabbage. Selected toppings and ingredients are added which can vary greatly (anything from meat and seafood to wasabi and cheese). This variability is reflected in the dish's name; "okonomi" literally means "to one's liking".

Mainly associated with Kansai and Hiroshima areas, Okonomiyaki is found throughout Japan. We offer three of the most popular styles: (1) Toshima, (2) Shibuya, and (3) Kita that you can order as is, or customize to your liking. We use a classic recipe for our dough and only the freshest ingredients from the local market. We offer a variety of sauces including traditional okonomiyaki sauce, terriyaki sauce, and tonkatsu sauce.

Choose from traditional combinations, to expertly made concoction of our own. If not of these work for you, why not make your own by mixing and adding ingredients onto a pizza from our menu or start from scratch.
