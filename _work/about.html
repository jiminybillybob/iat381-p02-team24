About

Tokyo Pizza Club was established in 2014 to offer Canadians a taste of the Japanese soul food, Okonomiyaki—a Japanese savoury pancake, but in a more familiar form. Mainly associated with Kansai and Hiroshima areas, Okonomiyaki is found throughout Japan with varying combinations of ingredients. We offer three of the most popular styles: (1) Monjayaki, (2) Osaka, and (3) Hiroshima that you can order as is, or customize to your liking.
